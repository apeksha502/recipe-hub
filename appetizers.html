Appetizer Recipes

1.Bruschetta

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 2 medium tomatoes (diced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Fresh basil leaves (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Toast the baguette slices until golden brown.

2. In a bowl, mix diced tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper.

3. Spoon the mixture onto the toasted baguette slices and serve immediately.

2.Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp parsley (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and finely chop them.

3. In a bowl, mix the chopped stems, cream cheese, Parmesan, breadcrumbs, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.

4. Stuff the mushroom caps with the mixture and place them on a baking sheet.

5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the mushrooms are tender and the filling is golden brown.
